## VIDEO 1 — "The Blob Test"
**Piattaforma:** Instagram Reel + TikTok
**Durata:** 25-30 secondi | **Formato:** 9:16 verticale
**Scopo:** Hook virale, primo contatto col prodotto
**Tono:** diretto, curioso, zero gergo

### Script

[0-3s] HOOK VISIVO — schermo nero, testo bianco grande:
"Can you hear 4 voices in this chord?"

[3-7s] AUDIO A — suona un Cmaj7 tutto al piano (preset Orchestra o Jazz Combo)
Screen recording dell'app: pentagramma con note, bottone PLAY premuto.
VOICEOVER: "This is a major seventh chord on piano. Most people hear... one sound."

[7-10s] TRANSIZIONE — breve pausa, testo overlay:
"Now listen again."

[10-18s] AUDIO B — stesso Cmaj7 con preset Clear Mix (timbri separati)
Screen recording: stessa vista, contrabbasso + tromba + corno + flauto separatamente.
VOICEOVER: "Same chord. But now each voice has its own instrument. Bass. Third. Fifth. Seventh. Four voices you can actually follow."

[18-22s] DEMO SOLO — tap sui bottoni Solo uno alla volta
Screen recording: tap su Bass (contrabbasso), tap su Lead (flauto). 2 sec per bottone.
VOICEOVER: "And you can isolate any voice."

[22-27s] CHIUSURA
Testo overlay: "ClearVoicing — Hear every voice inside the chord."
Sotto: "Free · Link in bio"
VOICEOVER: "ClearVoicing. Free. Link in bio."

### Note di produzione
- Il contrasto A/B (sec 3-7 vs 10-18) è il momento chiave
- Registra audio con screen recording iOS (cattura audio di sistema)
- Sottotitoli obbligatori — 70% dei Reel visti senza audio

## VIDEO 4 — "Follow the Voice"
**Piattaforma:** YouTube Short + Instagram Reel + TikTok

**Scopo:** dimostra il voice leading orizzontale
**Tono:** educational, "mind-blowing moment"

### Script

[0-3s] HOOK:
"Can you hear the third move in a ii-V-I?"

[3-10s] Progressione ii-V-I in Do (Dm7 - G7 - Cmaj7) con Clear Mix
Screen recording: pentagramma con progressione, note colorate.
VOICEOVER: "Two-five-one in C major. Three chords. Sounds like three separate events, right?"

[10-15s] Tap SOLO sulla voce "3rd"
Terza del Dm7 (F) → terza del G7 (B) → terza del Cmaj7 (E).
VOICEOVER: "Now listen to just the third voice. F... B... E. It's a melody hiding inside the chords."

VOICEOVER: "Now hear all four voices again. But this time... you can follow each one."

[28-33s] Ripete la progressione — nessun voiceover, solo musica

[33-40s] CHIUSURA

## VIDEO 6 — "Product Demo"
**Piattaforma:** YouTube
**Durata:** 3-5 minuti | **Formato:** 16:9 orizzontale
**Scopo:** demo completa per chi vuole capire prima di provare
**Tono:** personale, autentico, prima persona

### Script

[0-15s] INTRO
Voiceover su splash screen dell'app.
"When I play a chord on piano, I used to hear one sound. Not four voices — just one blob. I tried every ear training app out there. They all told me to listen harder. So I built something different."

[15-45s] IL PROBLEMA
Screen recording: suona un Dm7 tutto piano.
"Here's a D minor seventh on piano. Four notes. Can you pick out the third? The seventh? If you can — great, this video isn't for you. If you hear one mass of sound... welcome. That's exactly where I was."

[45-1:30] LA SOLUZIONE
Apri ClearVoicing. Suona lo stesso Dm7 con Clear Mix.
"ClearVoicing gives each voice a different instrument. Bass on double bass. The third on trumpet. The seventh on flute. Your brain already knows how to separate different timbres — it's the same reason you can hear a singer over a band. I'm just applying that principle to chord analysis."

[1:30-2:30] DEMO FEATURES
Mostra in sequenza:
- 3 preset timbrici (Clear Mix → Jazz Combo → Orchestra)
- Bottoni Solo (isola Bass, poi Lead, poi tutto)
- Codifica colori sul pentagramma
- REVEAL senza penalità
- Sessione di 3 challenge (velocizzata 2x se serve)
"No timers. Replay as many times as you need. The answer buttons are there when you're ready, not before."

[2:30-3:30] VOICE LEADING
Modalità Progression. Suona un ii-V-I.
"But the real power is in progressions. Instead of guessing chord labels, you learn to follow how each voice moves. This is voice leading — the way arrangers actually think about harmony. Solo the third voice... hear how it resolves by half step? That's the sound of tension resolving. And once you hear it, you can't unhear it."

[3:30-4:00] CHIUSURA
"ClearVoicing is free during the beta. No account required. Open it in your browser and start listening. Link in the description. If it helps you hear harmony differently, I'd love to know — drop a comment."
Testo finale: URL dell'app, logo, tagline.
