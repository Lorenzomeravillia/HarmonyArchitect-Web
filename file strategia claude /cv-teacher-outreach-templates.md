## TEMPLATE B — English (per insegnanti internazionali)

**Subject:** An ear training tool that separates chord voices by timbre — looking for teacher feedback

**Body:**

Hi [NAME],

I'm Lorenzo, a musician and developer. I built an ear training tool that takes a different approach and I'd love feedback from an experienced teacher.

The problem I tried to solve: when students hear a chord on piano, most perceive one sound — not four separate voices. I found that assigning a different instrument to each voice (double bass on root, trumpet on third, flute on seventh) allows the brain to separate them naturally, using the same principle that lets you follow a cello in an orchestra.

The tool is called ClearVoicing and it's free during the beta. It runs in the browser, no download needed:
[APP URL]

It includes:
- 4 harmony levels (triads through jazz extensions with tritone subs)
- 3 timbre presets (max separation → orchestral blend)
- Voice isolation (solo any voice by role)
- Color-coded staff notation by harmonic function
- Zero timers, zero penalties — students answer when ready

Would you be open to trying it with a few students and sharing your honest feedback? I'm especially interested in observations from teachers who work with harmony and ear training daily.

No commercial commitment — it's a free beta and I'm looking for real feedback to improve the product.

Thank you for your time,
Lorenzo

## NOTE OPERATIVE

### Dove trovare gli insegnanti:
- Siti web di scuole di musica italiane (Saint Louis Roma, CPM Milano, Siena Jazz, Lizard, Accademia del Suono)
- LinkedIn: cerca "insegnante armonia jazz" o "docente ear training"
- Instagram: cerca hashtag #armoniamusicalejazz #insegnantemusica #eartrainingitalia
- Conservatori statali: pagine docenti online

### Personalizzazione obbligatoria:
- Nome del destinatario
- Riferimento specifico alla scuola o al contesto ("ho visto che insegna armonia al CPM...")
- Se il destinatario ha un canale YouTube, menzionarlo ("ho visto il suo video su...")

### Tracking:
- Crea un foglio Excel con: Nome | Email | Scuola | Data invio | Risposta | Follow-up | Risultato
- Non mandare più di 10 email al giorno (evita spam filter)
- Personalizza OGNI email — niente copia-incolla identico
